Instructor

Nick, a Hawaiian native and former baseball player, is a knowledgeable yoga teacher who can teach traditional styles of yoga in an accessible way for everyone. He holds a black belt in Taekwondo and credits his success in sports to his yoga practice. Nick received his 500-hour RYT certificate from LongWave Yoga Studio in Wilmington, NC, and continues to study and practice yoga daily. He believes that everyone is a student of life and that we are all connected in this universe.

Practice Brings Peace

Ohana Yoga emphasizes a sense of community and family, with a focus on finding balance and inner peace. Regular practice can help build strength, flexibility, and relaxation skills, while also fostering a sense of belonging. By incorporating elements of mindfulness, practitioners are encouraged to be present and fully engaged in each moment, which can lead to a greater sense of peace and tranquility in daily life.

Amazing Events

Amazing yoga events, such as bonfire yoga and Namaste and wine, provide a new and exciting way to experience yoga. By combining the practice of yoga with fun, immersive experiences, participants can enjoy the benefits of yoga while also having a good time with like-minded individuals. Whether it's practicing yoga under the stars or indulging in a wine tasting after a session, these events offer a refreshing change of pace to the traditional yoga studio experience.
